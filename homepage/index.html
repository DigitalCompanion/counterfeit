Applications

ImageSpace

ImageSpace provides the ability to analyze and search through large numbers of images. These images may be text searched based on associated metadata and OCR text or a new image may be uploaded as a foundation for a search.

GeoParser

is an open source tool that can process information from any file or solr index, extract geographic coordinates, and visualize locations on a map.

Banana

Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive D3 charts and custom searches. Included features: make your own query, custom date range and filter top results. Exploratory analysis on collected advertisements and images from the Counterfeit Electronics domain.

ElectronicsViz

Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive D3 charts and custom searches. Included features: make your own query, custom date range and filter top results. Exploratory analysis on collected advertisements and images from the Counterfeit Electronics domain.

FacetView

Explore and visualize large data sets through interactive dashboards and custom searches. Included features: highlight terms, save searches and easily make your custom dashboards.
